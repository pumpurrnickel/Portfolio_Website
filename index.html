Hi, I'm Denise!

Nice to meet you!

Let's get to know each other!

I'm originally from the Philippines, but I moved to the United States after my parents found work in New York City. My dad would often bring me to his architecture firm, and I started to foster a love for the arts whenever he'd let me play with his watercolor sets. I graduated from North Carolina State University with a bachelor's in Art + Design, specializing in creating 3D models, animations, and environments for games.

Though I do a lot of digital work, I am most comfortable working with traditional paints such as watercolor and gouache. I love creating pieces that tell a rich narrative, which is why I love to render environments and focusing on the tiny details.
