Although I've been playing piano for years, I still don't know how to read sheet music. I mostly play songs by ear. I can also play the clarinet, ukulele, and lyre. I've been told I'm a good singer.

I like to play a variety of games, but I'm a fiend for rhythm games. My favorite one right now is 'Taiko no Tatsujin' on the Nintendo Switch, but I also like playing 'Osu!' or 'Friday Night Funkin'.

I make miniatures as one of my other side hobbies. I've been working on making buildings from the game 'Animal Crossing: New Horizons'. As of right now, I have made the Able Sister's shop and Nook's Cranny with working miniature lights inside

I sometimes stream games on Twitch for fun. I've also done Twitch emotes, overlays, and 'PNGtuber' character commissions for other Twitch streamers.

I like keeping a small drawing sketchbook to draw my surrounding environments and people that pass by, mainly to keep practicing composition and human anatomy.
